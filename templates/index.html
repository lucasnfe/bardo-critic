Music Generation with Emotion Experiment

Welcome to the Music Generation with Emotion Experiment!

PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION CAREFULLY BEFORE PROCEEDING.


WHO WE ARE:
We are Computer Science researchers at UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, SANTA CRUZ.


WHY WE ARE DOING THIS RESEARCH:
We are performing this experiment to test our new Artificial Intelligence (AI) method to automatically generate piano pieces with a given emotion.


WHAT YOU WILL HAVE TO DO:
In this experiment you will have to listen to a sequence of 5 piano pieces. Each piece has a single emotion transition. After listening to each piece, you will evaluate:

  1. The emotions perceived before and after the transition in that piece.
  2. The compositional quality of that piece.

PRIVACY AND DATA COLLECTION:
We will not ask you for your name or your identity.


DURATION:
You will evaluate 5 piano pieces in total. Each piece is approximately 30 seconds long. The entire experiment takes approximately 10 MINUTES.


NOTES:
We strongly recommend the use of headphones in this experiment.
